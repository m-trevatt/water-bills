## What is deliberately not here

No claim that Southern Water has committed fraud. No accusation against any named person. No citation of figures that couldn't be traced to a primary document within one step.

There are stronger arguments available to anyone willing to rely on secondary reporting. Boycott Water Bills uses them. This site doesn't, because its value rests on being unchallengeable on the facts.

## Refresh cadence

Water company rates change on 1 April each year. MPs change at general elections and by-elections, but the MP lookup on this site is live against Parliament's own API, so no editing is needed there. Everything else is audited quarterly.
